Professional Training & Overseas Investment

WOWChieve Intl, in partnership with various universities and colleges, professional associations, and companies in Florida, provides professional and executive training programs to Chinese clients. The training topics include a broad range of professional fields, such as hospitality/tourism management, early childhood education, teachers’ career advancement training, and entertainment management. We help our professionals develop core competencies that will empower them to manage the future training needs of their organizations. Our clients will earn a specialized focus on the role of the business leader and the skills needed to manage business and training performance. They will gain new tools and abilities to demonstrate their expertise in training management and to push their career in training and development to the next level.

In addition, WOWChieve provides consulting services to Chinese companies, schools, and individuals to assist their overseas investment targeting at the areas of strategic partnerships, real estate development, and education-related project investment. We will help empower our Chinese customers with a comprehensive list of property listings, new features, market analysis and property buying guides to assist them in making informed and educated decisions about purchasing overseas properties.
