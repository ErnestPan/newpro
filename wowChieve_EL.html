Taking the Experiential Learning approach, which is a process via which students develop knowledge, skills, and values from direct experiences outside a traditional academic setting, WOWChieve Intl. aims to foster “global minded” students and teachers via authentic hands-on learning system and the lenses of American educational ideology. WOWChieve Intl. is a spokesman of the abundant tourism resources in Orlando and Florida.

By offering short-term “study abroad” programs, workshops, learning tours, winter and summer camps, our well-planned, supervised, and assessed experiential learning program can stimulate academic inquiries; meanwhile, the experiential learning program, supported by our advanced academic and technological platforms, is believed to promote career development, cultural awareness, leadership, and other professional and intellectual skills. WOWChieve Intl. , essentially, is to broaden views of the students from both China and US, increase their adaptedness to different cultures, and prepare them to study and work in a foreign country.
