Recruiting Teachers to Teach in China

The True China Cultural Exchange program, in which we partner with GIE, offers services to foreigners who have a desire to travel to China whether it is to study, teach, or explore. We help international individuals come to China and teach in public and private schools. The program, called Work in China, seeks talented individuals looking to work as a foreign teacher, an academic advisor or an intern. The True China Cultural Exchange program also offers Explore and Study Tours for groups that are interested in traveling throughout China, learning Mandarin, and exploring Chinese culture.

We believe, teachers choosing to join the True China Cultural Exchange program are to obtain prestigious teaching opportunities, shine themselves upon our transforming platforms, and achieve cultural exchange via interactions with our students and staff.

DETAILS of SUBSIDY

Our teacher's subsidy is well above the average wage in China. All of our teachers are able to live comfortably with the subsidy given monthly and many save for future travels around Asia. For individuals with higher credentials and experience, our subsidy increases. For individuals that sign a long term contract, the subsidy will also increase.
