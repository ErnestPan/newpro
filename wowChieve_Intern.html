Career Development Center

We assist current and graduating students in paid and unpaid internships in the U.S. It can be a great way to experience local companies that student may have difficulty gaining access to. With our counsel and guidance, the process of finding internships is much easier for students, and often we are able to suit students with the best placements, usually aligning with their resume and interviews. Systematically, our professionally-designed portfolio links students to a company that can offer them the experience they need.

In addition, we also offer services such as accommodation, airport pickup, on-going support throughout the internship, and a completion certificate. Other benefits of using our internship placement program are: internships based on students' preferences, mentoring, certificate of achievement, discounts, and offers with local providers.

Every year, 800 new college students come to the U.S. and study in the major universities through GIE. As of May, 2017, GIE has 3000 undergraduate and graduate students studying in US universities and colleges. More than 50% of them study in U.S. top 50 schools. WOWChieve Intl. seeks and partners with major employers to provide pay or non-paid internship to the students using OPT and CPT approved by US immigration department.
